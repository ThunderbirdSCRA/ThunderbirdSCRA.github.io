Privacy Policy

The Thunderbird Chapter is committed to protecting the privacy of its members, visitors, and users. We believe that privacy is a fundamental right, and we uphold this principle by maintaining the following policies:

1. Data Collection
The Thunderbird Chapter does not collect, store, or retain any personal data from users. We do not track, monitor, or analyze user activity on our website or any digital platforms. 

2. Data Sharing
Since we do not collect any user data, we do not sell, share, or provide any user information to third parties, including advertisers, marketing agencies, or external organizations.

3. Security Measures
As we do not retain any user data, there is no risk of unauthorized access, theft, or misuse of personal information. Any engagement with our chapter is conducted with full confidentiality.

4. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to external sites. Please note that these third-party sites operate independently and may have different privacy practices. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit.

5. Policy Updates
We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed to reflect any changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any revisions will be posted on our website with an updated effective date.

6. Acceptance
By accessing and using the Thunderbird Chapter website, users acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of these terms.

7. Contact Information
For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please reach out to us at: help@thunderbirdscra.org
